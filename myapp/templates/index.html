Problem: Inconsistency Detection in Medical Annotation

Problem Code: RG1

College Code: 1-3516083173

Idea

  • Use Mesh topology to find similar terms in the corpus which are differently annotated(eg: Hypertension and High blood pressure are synonyms but could be annotated differently leaving inconsistency).
  • Terms in each document are mapped to the entry terms in MeSH, which are further then mapped into MeSH descriptors to remove synonyms.
  • Instead of searching all entry terms in the MeSH against each document, we select 1-gram to 5-gram words as the candidates of MeSH Entry terms.
  • Implement the Watson Natural Language Understanding API, to extract Concepts from the entities(eg: High blood pressure has Hypertension as concept). Grouping similar entities using MeSH and Watson Natural Language Understanding API based on their semantic similarity.
  • Finding out weight discrepancy to extract the extent of inconsistency for each grouped entity i.e. difference between the number of times an expression M occurs is annotated minus the number of times M occurs is not annotated.
  • More is the weight discrepancy lesser is the extent of inconsistency
